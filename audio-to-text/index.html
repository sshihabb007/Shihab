⚡ How to Use

  1. Click ⚙️ Settings (top right) to configure your AI.
  2. Enter your Google AI Studio or Groq API Key.
  3. Select your preferred Transcription Engine.
  4. Drag & Drop any audio file (MP3, WAV, M4A).
  5. Click Copy Text when the transcription finishes!

✨ Key Features

  • 👉 Gemini 2.5 Flash-Lite: Deep multimodal analysis for massive files (including songs).
  • 👉 Groq Whisper V3: Lightning-fast hardware processing for files under 25MB.
  • 👉 100% Private Local AI: Fallback to your browser's WebGPU engine with zero cloud sharing.
  • 👉 Live Progress: Real-time download, chunking, and generation tracking.
